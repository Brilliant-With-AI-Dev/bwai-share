Grounded vs. proposed-new

  • NEW The mic pill, dropdown, mobile status-colors, bottom-sheet, and the strings “Choose microphone” / “Test microphone…”.
  • REUSE Forest --accent #157a4e, --navy-*, --off-white, the .user-float-trigger 32px gear spec, the MicTestModal + onDismiss(result, deviceId) contract, setMicDeviceId / getEffectiveMicDeviceId, and the existing “Set up / Re-test microphone” copy.
  • Visibility (D1): rendered only when useMicDevicePersistenceFlag() === true.
  • Pre-permission (D2): labels are empty until a getUserMedia grant, so before permission the pill reads “Set up microphone” and opens the MicTestModal rather than a name-less dropdown.
  • Mobile: the center “Mirror” label hides <640px; the pill collapses to an icon whose color is the status (forest=tested · navy=default · rose=blocked); the picker is a thumb-reachable bottom sheet.
