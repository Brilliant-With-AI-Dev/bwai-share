This shows where the button lands — and the case that earns the product its "it remembers me" reputation: she taps Resume after twelve days away. There is no welcome screen to clear. She drops back exactly where she left off — same scroll, her half-typed reply still in the box, the side conversation reopened. The one new thing is a recap that thickens because she's been gone: last time · open loops · next, sitting above the work, never in front of it. The full archive is a deliberate second click, never the landing. The assistant stays unnamed.
