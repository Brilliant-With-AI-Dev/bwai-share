Act 2 · How I've changeda reflection, honestly caveated

How have I changed? — honest, caveated, no scores.

No numbers, no percentages, no confidence meters, no dashboards. Moe opens by saying exactly what this is — what they're hearing across your conversations, not a measurement — and then lays out a few honest then → now pairs, each grounded in something you actually said and clear about what's inferred versus told. It reads like a person who's been paying attention, not an analytics page.
